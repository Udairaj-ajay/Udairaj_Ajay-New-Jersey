  • What is the average temperature in New Jersey?

    +

    Depending on the season this is the average temperature Winter (December to February): Average temperatures range from about 25°F to 35°F, with January being the coldest month. Spring (March to May): Temperatures gradually rise from around 45°F in March to 70°F in May. Summer (June to August): This is the warmest season, with average temperatures typically between the high 80s°F and high 90s°F. Fall (September to November): Temperatures start to cool down, averaging around 68°F in September and dropping to 47°F by November.

  • What is the best season to visit New Jersey?

    +

    The best times to visit New Jersey are during the spring (April to June) and fall (September to October). During these months, the weather is mild and pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 59°F to 77°F (15°C to 25°C). Spring brings blooming flowers and sunny days, while fall offers beautiful foliage and comfortable temperatures without the summer crowds. If you enjoy festivals and outdoor activities, these seasons are ideal for exploring everything New Jersey has to offer.
