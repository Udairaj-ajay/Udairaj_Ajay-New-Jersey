What is New Jersey known for?

One thing known about New jersey is their incredible beaches and board walks .New Jersey’s most famous boardwalk is the Atlantic City Boardwalk. It’s the oldest and longest boardwalk in the world, offering stunning views of the beach, a variety of shops, and numerous activities like fishing and windsurfing



Sandy Hook

A seven-mile of barrier beach spit, Sandy Hook’s pristine, undeveloped shorelines provide an opportunity to explore and enjoy a natural beach habitat for shorebirds and unique plant life. Visitors can enjoy swimming, sunbathing, fishing, surfing.



6 flags

Six Flags is a chain of amusement parks with 27 parks across the United States, Mexico and Canada1. The name "Six Flags" originally referred to the flags of the six different nations that have governed Texas: Spain, France, Mexico, the Republic of Texas, the United States of America, and the Confederate States of America.
